Description

The Ministry of Tourism HR Management System is a web-based solution designed to manage employee data efficiently and transparently. The system encompasses employee data management, digital attendance, leave management, performance evaluation, and administrative reports that support data-driven decision making.

With this system, previously manual HR processes have become faster, more accurate, and integrated, helping to improve work efficiency within the Ministry of Tourism environment.
