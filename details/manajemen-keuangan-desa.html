Description

The Village Financial Management System is a web-based solution developed to help village governments manage their finances transparently, accountably, and efficiently. The application covers budget planning, income & expense recording, and financial reporting in compliance with government regulatory standards.

Before this system was implemented, village financial records were maintained manually, which was prone to errors and lacked transparency. With this system, every financial transaction can be recorded automatically and monitored in real-time to enhance accountability.
